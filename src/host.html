Host

The 14th annual Sir Bani Yas Forum is hosted by the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs, with the Atlantic Council serving as the Ministry’s substantive adviser for the Forum.

Since its founding, the United Arab Emirates has embraced the principles of peaceful co-existence, mutual respect, and non-interference in other countries’ internal affairs as cornerstones of the country’s foreign policy.

Over the years the Ministry has partnered with various think tanks offering substantive guidance to support the Forum. The Atlantic Council provides the Sir Bani Yas Forum substantive advice, driven by its central mission of “shaping the global future together.”
