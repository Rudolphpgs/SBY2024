Mission Statement

Bringing the world together to address the Middle East’s most important challenges and opportunities

The Sir Bani Yas Forum brings together diverse leaders of politics, thought, and business to explore how the Middle East can both navigate global pressures and affect global trends.

During the annual nonattributable retreat, the Forum’s participants dedicate themselves to developing creative policy options for navigating the region’s most pressing challenges, and search for new opportunities to create a more prosperous future for all.
