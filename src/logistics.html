Forum Location
8 - 10 December 2023

The 2023 SBY Forum will convene at Jumeirah at Saadiyat Island Resort.

The resort will be closed to public during the dates of the Forum, and entry will be granted only to registered guests of the forum with SBY ID badges (please see below).

Airport Transfers

All guests of the Sir Bani Yas Forum will be personally received and escorted to the airport VIP Lounge for immigration and baggage collection, then taken to their limousine transfer to Jumeirah at Saadiyat Island Resort. On departure, cars will be booked according to guest departure flight times and guests will be informed in advance.

Approximate journey times between the airport and the Jumeirah at Saadiyat Island Resort are: Abu Dhabi (AUH): 35 minutes Dubai (DXB): 90 minutes

Registration & Access

The Sir Bani Yas Forum is a private event by invitation only, and neither online nor on-site registration is available for the event. All registrations are handled in advance by the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Please contact sby@mofa.gov.ae should you wish to discuss a registration for the event.

Forum ID badge registrations are available in advance to:

  • Invited guests
  • Spouse of invited guest
  • One travelling assistant (a non-resident of the UAE) to accompany a minister-level attendee.

SBY Forum Access by Badge Type

Please note that only guests with a Delegate ID badge will be permitted entry to the Forum Conference Room. Spouse and Advisor ID badge holders are welcome to listen to the conference proceedings from the Listening Room.

Dress Code

We welcome all guests to wear business casual or national dress during all events of the Sir Bani Yas Forum, including the dinners.

Hotel Accommodation & Billing

Invited guests will be provided accommodation at the Jumeirah at Saadiyat Island Resort.

Bookings will be made directly by the SBY Forum with the hotel, according to arrival and departure schedules. Upon arrival, guests will be greeted by protocol staff who will provide guests with their room key and Forum ID badge (which should be worn at all times).

Check-in from 13:00, Thursday 7th December, Check-out before 16:00, Monday 11th December.

The SBY Forum will cover the room charges for invited guests between 7th and 11th December. In addition, the Forum will also cover laundry, in-room dining, beverage and dining in outlets located in the Jumeirah at Saadiyat Island Resort.

Guests will be billed for any of the following exclusions:

  • Spa treatments in excess of spa voucher allocation
  • Retail purchases
